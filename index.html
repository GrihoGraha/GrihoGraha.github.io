About Grihograha

Visionary Design, Flawless Execution.

Grihograha was founded by Saneul Ali Khan with a singular mission: to transform creative visions into breathtaking, tangible realities. We are a modern design studio specializing in high-fidelity 3D visualization for both interior and exterior spaces.

With years of experience in civil engineering and a passion for aesthetic detail, we bridge the gap between blueprint and beauty, allowing clients to experience their future homes and commercial spaces before a single brick is laid.

Our Pricing

Transparent pricing for our design services. All prices are indicative and may vary based on project complexity.

3D Services

Exterior Design

  • Residential G+1 (up to 1000 sqft): 8,000 - 10,000
  • Commercial G+1 (up to 1000 sqft): 10,000 - 13,000
  • Extra Floor: +2,000 (Res), +3,000 (Com)

Interior Design

  • Residential: 23 per sqft
  • Commercial: 25 per sqft

2D Services

Includes: Planning, Section, Column/Beam Detailing, Column Layout, Slab Detailing

  • Residential G+1 (up to 1000 sqft): 5,000 - 8,000
  • Commercial G+1 (up to 1000 sqft): 8,000 - 10,000
  • Extra Floor: +1,000
  • Exception: Truss structure, Godown, Factory (custom quote)

Terms & Conditions

  • 25% Advanced amount to be paid beforehand.
  • 50% after 3D Modelling and before rendering.
  • Rest full amount to be paid at time of delivery.
  • Advanced payment is non-refundable.

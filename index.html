About GrihoGraha

Visionary Design, Flawless Execution.

GrihoGraha was founded by Saneul Ali Khan with a singular mission: to transform creative visions into breathtaking, tangible realities. We are a modern design studio specializing in high-fidelity 3D visualization for both interior and exterior spaces.

With years of experience in civil engineering and a passion for aesthetic detail, we bridge the gap between blueprint and beauty, allowing clients to experience their future homes and commercial spaces before a single brick is laid.

Terms & Conditions

Client Responsibilities
  • Clients must provide complete and accurate information, including design plans, materials, textures, measurements, and references.
  • Delays in providing necessary information may result in extended delivery timelines.
Project Timeline & Delivery
  • Estimated delivery time is discussed at project initiation.
  • Final files will be shared in high resolution (JPEG/PNG/MP4 or as agreed).
  • Delays caused by client revisions or late feedback will not be held against GRIHO GRAHA.
Revisions Policy
  • Includes 3 free rounds of minor revisions.
  • Major revisions or additional rounds beyond this will incur extra charges.
  • Change requests must be made within 7 days of initial delivery.
Payment Terms
  • 50% advance payment to begin work; remaining 50% upon final delivery.
  • No refunds once the project is started.
Copyright & Usage
  • We retain the right to display finished works in our portfolio or for marketing unless a separate NDA is signed.
  • Clients may use the visuals only for the agreed-upon purposes (e.g., client presentations, marketing).
  • Unauthorized resale, reproduction, or modification is prohibited.
Confidentiality
  • All client data and design materials will be kept confidential.
  • Signing of NDAs available upon requests.
